🤖 About the AI

Welcome to the future of sports analytics. This platform runs completely autonomously, leveraging the advanced Machine Learning engine of API-Football. Every morning, the AI scans global fixtures, calculates precise probabilities for Match Winners (1X2) and Goals (Over/Under), and compares them against live bookmaker odds to detect hidden Value Bets 🌟. Every night, it grades its own performance to ensure maximum transparency. No human intervention, just pure data-driven edges.

Predictions are based on statistical models and historical data. Not financial advice.
